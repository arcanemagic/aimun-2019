Message from the Chairperson

Dear Delegates,

I extend a warm welcome to all the delegations from USA, Poland, Russia, Slovakia, Italy, Hungary, and across India participating in the 10th Chapter of Amity International Model United Nations. A decade of leaving behind a legacy, a decade of achieving new milestones and scaling new heights, a decade of nurturing leaders, and a decade of giving students all across the world a platform like AIMUN to hone their skills- AIMUN is the flagship international event of Amity Group of Schools and has today gained the honour of being such a largely celebrated event all across the globe.

I congratulate Amity Educational Resource Centre to make this a reality by successfully organizing AIMUN Dubai in 2017 and AIMUN Singapore in 2018. This year, the AIMUN conference has a wide range of enthralling and mesmeric agendas and committees, to ensure that every delegate has the chance to learn and grow, to discover the power of team work and to contribute towards making a better world. A better world will not be made by itself; it is up to the youth to take up the mantle of responsibility in order to bring about a difference. In this era of globalization, it is only the youth of today, who with their unfathomed potential to harbinger epochal change in the world, can be entrusted to lead us towards humanity and peace. With their innovative ideas, unwavering zeal, unparalleled commitment and boundless energy, they can harness the resources of the planet and shape its destiny in a better way.

Nelson Mandela once said, “A good leader can engage in a debate frankly and thoroughly, knowing that at the end he and the other side must be closer, and thus emerge stronger.” Concurring with this philosophy, we are nurturing the youth around the AIMUN universe. I pray that through this experience, each of you take away positive thoughts as expressed by the mantra ‘Krinvanto Vishvamaaryam’ meaning –Make world a better place. If we are able to achieve this, the world would be able to eliminate the misery and treason and be glorious again. I wish you all the best for this conference!

Dr. (Mrs.) Amita Chauhan
Chairperson
Amity Group of Schools

Message from the Chargé D'Affaires

Dear Delegates,

I take this opportunity to welcome you all to the 10th edition of Amity International Model United Nations, a chance for promising and eloquent diplomats to debate, discuss, and manifest ideas that transform the future of mankind. Ever since the inception of AIMUN in 2010, Amity has proudly held the conference under the guidance of our respected Chairperson Dr. Mrs. Amita Chauhan, as a stage for meaningful exchange of perspectives, as a powerful medium of expression for students- where they put forth their outlook on issues of global concern, and brainstorm ideas that truly change the world. The 10th edition is a reminder to look back at the path-breaking journey of immense hard work that has gone into making AIMUN a world-class conference which has globally expanded its wings over the last decade.

Along with stimulating debate, the conference gives students an opportunity to widen their horizons by interacting with young people from diverse cultures from across the world. Passionate delegates from all across the world have gathered with a resolve of hope, liberty, and justice. The young ambassadors breathe life into this way of life by working in unity to resolve the problems that humanity is posed with. We, at Amity International Model United Nations nurture the leaders of tomorrow who set themselves apart from the ordinary by aiming high and breaking the glass ceiling each time, for even the sky isn’t the limit.

The world around us is more dynamic today than ever before, and it is time to invoke within ourselves the tenets which bound us in the service of mankind. AIMUN bestows upon every student the chance to enunciate their stance, satiate their curiosities and raise their voices in a manner which leads to the constructive resolution of the issues at hand. It edifies in students the power of being able to put their beliefs to words and adapt those words into efficacious action. As you step into the shoes of a delegate and commence your journey spearheading with conviction, allow integrity, humility and compassion to lead you in the right direction. May the trail of footprints that you leave behind remind the future generation of their commitment towards their resolve- to make the world a better place.

Mrs. Jyoti Arora
Chargé D'Affaires, AIMUN
Director
Amity Educational Resource Centre
Amity Group of Schools
