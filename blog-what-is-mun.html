What even is an MUN?

MUN is a world class educational experience. Delegates take place of world leaders and discuss issues that are affecting people everyday life. Students come up with creative solutions while representing countries and negotiating problems with each other. MUNs work in transforming many students, it helps them to be more confident in public speaking and express themselves by improving communication skills. It is an amazing platform.

Mimansha Kumar

Class VIII, Amity International School, Saket
